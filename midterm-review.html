💬 Sentence Patterns (句型)

💬 句型複習
This / That is + 單數名詞 → This is a table. / That is a chair.
These / Those are + 複數名詞 → These are pencils. / Those are rulers.
Yes/No 問答：Are they happy? → Yes, they are. / No, they aren't.
Can he skateboard? → Yes, he can. / No, he can't.
Does he have milkshake? → Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.
Is this your book? → Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.

✏️ Proofreading (改錯)

✏️ 改錯 4 大規則
1. 句首大寫：this is my book. → This is my book.
2. 人名大寫：she is miss yvonne. → She is Miss Yvonne.
3. 星期大寫：We have art on tuesdays. → ...on Tuesdays.
4. 問句加問號：Is this a pen → Is this a pen?

🧤 The Lost Mitten (課文閱讀)

🧤 The Lost Mitten 課文
Lance gets new mittens. He goes out to find firewood.
But he loses a mitten! Mice find the mitten. They want to hide. They go inside.
Rabbit wants to sleep. He goes in with the mice.
Hedgehog is cold. He goes in with the mice and the rabbit.
Bear is tired. She goes in with the mice, the rabbit, and the hedgehog.
It's crowded in the mitten. Cricket sees his friends. He goes in, too.
Now it's too crowded! Rip! Snap! POP! Poor Lance! His mitten is gone!
