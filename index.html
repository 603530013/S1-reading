🐣 Fly Away! 🐦

Unit 1 複習 · 7歲小朋友版

📚 故事「Fly Away!」大綱

1

🪹 A mother and father bird make a nest.

鳥媽媽和鳥爸爸築一個巢。

2

🥚 The mother bird lays four eggs and keeps them warm.

鳥媽媽生了四顆蛋，讓牠們保持溫暖。

3

🐍 A snake wants to eat the eggs! The mother bird stops it with her beak.

蛇想吃蛋！鳥媽媽用嘴喙阻止牠。

4

🐣 The baby birds come out of the eggs. Parents give them worms.

小鳥從蛋裡出來了。爸媽給牠們吃蟲子。

5

🪶 Little birds grow feathers. Parents keep them safe.

小鳥長出羽毛。爸媽保護牠們安全。

6

🕊️ Soon the birds are big and strong. It's time to fly away!

小鳥長大又強壯，是時候飛走了！

💬 重要句子

Mother birds lay eggs.
鳥媽媽生蛋。
When baby birds grow feathers, they leave the nest.
小鳥長出羽毛後，牠們離開鳥巢。
Little birds fly away when they are strong.
小鳥長強壯了就飛走了。
I grow and get smarter / taller / bigger / stronger.
我成長並且變得更聰明/更高/更大/更強壯。
