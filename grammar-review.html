例：John can swim → He can swim.

🔤 Unit 14 · "Am", "Is" and "Are" (be動詞)

這三個是 "to be" 動詞的形式
Am 用於 I
Is 用於 she, he 和單數名詞
Are 用於 you, they, we 和複數名詞
例：I am a pupil. / She is a doctor. / They are teachers.

🕐 Unit 17 · Simple Present Tense (簡單現在式)

簡單現在式用於描述習慣性動作或一般事實
動詞有兩種形式：base form 和 base + s
I / You / We / They / 複數名詞 → 用 base form（原形）
She / He / It / 單數名詞 → 用 base + s（加 s）
例：speak → speaks, run → runs, like → likes, go → goes

▶️ Unit 18 · Present Continuous Tense (現在進行式)

現在進行式用於描述正在發生的動作
結構：am / is / are + 動詞 -ing（現在分詞）
I → am + -ing / She, He, It → is + -ing / You, We, They → are + -ing
例：I am writing the answer. / The baby is sleeping soundly.
例：The boys are waiting for their parents.

📍 Unit 19 · Prepositions (介系詞)

介系詞用於表示位置、地點或移動方向
位置介系詞：on (在上面), in (在裡面), above (在上方), under (在下面), in front of (在前面), behind (在後面)
移動介系詞：to (到), from (從)
例：The book is on the chair. / The stick is in the box.

🔗 Unit 20 · Conjunctions (連接詞)

連接詞是用來連接單字或句子的詞
and：連接相似或附加的內容（兩者都有）
or：連接選項（二選一）
例：Mrs Lee has a daughter and a son. (兩個都有)
例：Is this a pen or a pencil? (選一個)

❓ Unit 21 · Question Words (疑問詞)

疑問詞用來詢問資訊
Who：詢問人名（Who is this? This is Alan.）
What：詢問事物、活動或職業（What is this? It is a rabbit.）
例：What is his job? He is a postman.
例：Who are they? They are Alan and Mary.

🚫 Unit 22 · Negative and Affirmative Statements (否定句與肯定句)

肯定句 (Affirmative) 是正面的句子
否定句 (Negative) 含有 not
例：This is an umbrella. → This is not a stick.
例：They like balloons. → They do not like balloons.
例：I have a pen. → I do not have a pen.
