👤 Unit 1 · Singular Pronouns (單數代名詞)

單數代名詞：I, you, he, she（一個人或一個東西）
代名詞 (pronoun) 取代「名詞」
例：John can swim → He can swim.
例：My mother is tired. She needs a rest.

🌟 Unit 1-11 全部複習 (綜合練習)

🎯 Unit 1-11 綜合複習
代名詞：單數 (I, you, he, she)、複數 (we, you, they)
反身代名詞：myself/yourself/himself/herself/itself/ourselves/yourselves/themselves
指示代名詞：this/that/these/those（近↔遠、單↔複）
名詞：a/an、單數/複數規則、不可數、永遠複數 (spectacles, trousers, pyjamas)
形容詞：放名詞前 (a fat cat) 或 be 動詞後 (It is big.)
