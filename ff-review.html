📐 文法重點：This / That / These / Those

近 vs 遠 · 單數 vs 複數
單數（1個）複數（2個以上）
近的（here） This is a computer.
這是一台電腦。		 These are tables.
這些是桌子。
遠的（there） That is a board.
那是一個黑板。		 Those are coat hooks.
那些是衣鉤。
❗ 記住
  • 1
    This / That + is（單數）
    This is my book. / That is a pen.
  • 2
    These / Those + are（複數）
    These are chairs. / Those are bags.

📐 文法重點：現在進行式 What are ___ doing?

問正在做什麼 + 回答
問句回答
What are you doing?I'm dancing.
我正在跳舞。
What are we doing?We're singing.
我們正在唱歌。
What is he/she doing?He's/She's eating.
他/她正在吃東西。
What are they doing?They're making a cake.
他們正在做蛋糕。
疑問句：Are they ___ing? / Is he/she ___ing?
問句肯定否定
Are they dancing?Yes, they are.No, they aren't.
Is she sleeping?Yes, she is.No, she isn't.
❗ -ing 規則
  • 1
    一般：直接加 -ing
    eat → eating, sing → singing, drink → drinking
  • 2
    字尾 e 去掉再加 -ing
    dance → dancing, make → making, ride → riding

📐 文法重點：比較級 Comparatives

bigger / smaller / taller / shorter than
原形比較級例句
bigbigger thanA horse is bigger than a goose.
smallsmaller thanA goose is smaller than a horse.
talltaller thanRosy is taller than Billy.
shortshorter thanBilly is shorter than Rosy.
loudlouder thanThe goose is louder than the hen.
fastfaster thanThe dog is faster than the cat.
❗ 規則
  • 1
    短形容詞 + -er + than
    tall → taller, fast → faster, loud → louder
  • 2
    字尾重複子音：big → bigger
    big → bigger, fat → fatter

📐 文法重點：There were some / There weren't any

過去式 · some / any
句型例句中文
肯定There were some chairs.（過去）有一些椅子。
否定There weren't any tables.（過去）沒有任何桌子。
問句Were there any children?（過去）有孩子嗎？
❗ some vs any
  • ✓
    some：用在肯定句
    There were some women.
  • ✗
    any：用在否定句 & 問句
    There weren't any men. / Were there any children?
