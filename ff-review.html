📐 文法重點：This / That / These / Those

近 vs 遠 · 單數 vs 複數
單數（1個）複數（2個以上）
近的（here） This is a computer.
這是一台電腦。		 These are tables.
這些是桌子。
遠的（there） That is a board.
那是一個黑板。		 Those are coat hooks.
那些是衣鉤。
❗ 記住
  • 1
    This / That + is（單數）
    This is my book. / That is a pen.
  • 2
    These / Those + are（複數）
    These are chairs. / Those are bags.

📐 文法重點：be 動詞 + 感覺

We're / They're · Are...? · 回答
主詞肯定否定

📐 文法重點：What do/does ... like?

like / likes / doesn't like
主詞問句回答
youWhat do you like?I like balloons.
he / sheWhat does she like?She likes candy.
❗ 記住
  • 1
    I / You → like
    I like cake.
  • 2
    He / She → likes（要加 s）
    She likes cake.
  • 3
    He / She → doesn't like（否定）
    He doesn't like balloons.
