📚 Unit 7「Animals on the Go」

🐘 故事大綱（Nonfiction）
1

🐍 Snakes slither on the ground. They can move fast.

蛇在地上爬行，移動很快。

2

🐢 Turtles are slow on land. In water they can swim fast.

烏龜在陸地慢，在水中游得快。

3

🦀 Crabs crawl across the sand. Then they dig a hole and hide.

螃蟹爬過沙地，再挖洞躲起來。

4

🐛 Insects crawl too. Some can fly. They buzz around. They can be noisy.

昆蟲也會爬，有些會飛，嗡嗡叫，很吵。

5

🐘 Elephants stomp on the ground. They are noisy, too. Look at them wave their trunks!

大象跺腳也很吵，還會揮舞象鼻！

🗂️ Main Idea & Key Details
Main Idea：Animals move in many ways.
Snakes → slither | Crabs → crawl | Insects → buzz
Turtles → swim | Elephants → stomp

📚 Unit 8「A Tale of a Tail」

🦫 故事大綱（Folktale）
1

🦫 Beaver has a beautiful, fluffy tail. "I have the best tail of all!" he says.

河狸有美麗蓬鬆的尾巴，覺得自己尾巴最棒！

2

🪵 Beaver chops down a tree, but it falls on his tail. "My tail is flat!"

砍樹時樹倒下壓扁了尾巴！「我的尾巴變扁了！」

3

☀️ The sun says: "Your flat tail can help you swim fast!"

太陽說：扁尾巴可以幫你游得快！

4

🎢 Beaver invites friends: "Climb on my tail for a ride!"

河狸邀請朋友：爬上我的尾巴坐船！

5

🏊 Beaver carries his friends across the lake. Everyone likes Beaver's new flat tail!

河狸載著朋友過湖，大家都喜歡他的新尾巴！

🔗 Cause & Effect
Cause：Beaver chops down a tree → Effect：tail is flat
Cause：flat tail → Effect：Beaver can swim fast & carry friends

📚 Unit 9「A Team of Fish」

🐟 故事大綱（Nonfiction）
1

🌊 Fish swim in lakes, rivers, and the ocean. Fish can swim alone or with a partner.

魚住在湖、河和海洋，可以單獨游或結伴游。

2

🐠 Some fish swim close to each other in groups. They work in a team.

有些魚靠在一起成群，組成一個團隊。

3

🍽️ Fish in a team help each other. They search for food together.

團隊裡的魚互相幫助，一起搜尋食物。

4

⚠️ The sea can be dangerous for small fish. Big fish can eat them!

海洋對小魚很危險，大魚會吃牠們！

5

🐡 In a group, small fish can look like one big fish. Big fish leave them alone!

成群的小魚看起來像一條大魚，大魚就不敢靠近！

🗂️ Main Idea & Key Details
Main Idea：Working in a team helps fish!
They search for food together & stay safe from big fish.

📚 Unit 10「Hungry Animals」

🐆 故事大綱（Nonfiction）
1

🦛 All animals need food to live and grow. Hippos eat a lot of grass.

所有動物需要食物，河馬吃很多草。

2

🐿️ Squirrels eat seeds and nuts. They can smell nuts under the snow.

松鼠吃種子和堅果，能在雪地下找到堅果。

3

🐆 Wild animals like cheetahs hunt other animals. They can run very fast.

野生動物如獵豹獵食其他動物，跑得很快。

4

🐸 Frogs and toads catch bugs with their long tongues.

青蛙和蟾蜍用長舌頭捕捉蟲子。

5

🐻 Bears eat fish. They like plants, too. Some bears even eat people food!

熊吃魚也吃植物，有些熊甚至吃人的食物！

🗂️ Main Idea & Key Details
Main Idea：Animals eat different kinds of food.
Hippos → grass | Squirrels → nuts | Cheetahs → hunt animals
Frogs → catch bugs with tongues | Bears → fish & plants

📚 Unit 11「Bug Pals」

🦋 故事大綱（Fantasy）
1

🐛 Five bugs are playing in a field. Bee and Ladybug can fly. Grasshopper can jump. Ant can run fast.

五隻蟲子一起玩。瓢蟲和蜜蜂會飛，蚱蜢會跳，螞蟻跑得快。

2

😢 Poor Caterpillar can't fly or jump. He can't run fast. Caterpillar is sad. He creeps away.

毛毛蟲不會飛也不會跳，跑不快，很難過，悄悄爬走了。

3

🍱 The bugs have lunch. But where is Caterpillar?

大家吃午餐，但毛毛蟲不見了！

4

✨ One day, they see a beautiful bug with gold wings. "It's me! Caterpillar!" says Butterfly.

有一天，出現一隻有金色翅膀的美麗蟲子，「是我！毛毛蟲！」蝴蝶說。

5

🦋 "You're not the same," says Ant. "Yes, I'm different now. But I'm still your friend!"

「你變了！」但蝴蝶說：「我變了，但我還是你的朋友！」

👥 Characters & Events
Characters：Bee, Ladybug, Grasshopper, Ant, Caterpillar → Butterfly
Caterpillar creeps away → becomes a beautiful butterfly

📚 Unit 12「Helper Dogs」

🐕 故事大綱（Nonfiction）
1

👁️ Some people can't see. They get help from special dogs called guide dogs.

有些人看不見，牠們靠導盲犬幫助。

2

🐶 These dogs are calm, smart, and easy to train.

導盲犬個性冷靜、聰明、容易訓練。

3

📅 It takes two years to train a guide dog. First, the dog lives with a family for one year.

訓練導盲犬要兩年，第一年先住在一個家庭中。

4

🎓 Then a trainer teaches the dog many things: cross the street, stop at a red signal, lead a person through busy streets.

接著訓練師教牠穿越馬路、遇紅燈停下、引導人走過繁忙街道。

5

🌟 Guide dogs are great helpers!

導盲犬是很棒的幫手！

🗂️ Main Idea & Key Details
Main Idea：Guide dogs are great helpers.
They are calm & smart → easy to train → learn to lead people
