📚 Unit 1「Fly Away!」

🐣 故事大綱
1

🪹 A mother and father bird make a nest.

鳥媽媽和鳥爸爸築巢。

2

🥚 The mother bird lays four eggs and keeps them warm.

生四顆蛋，保持溫暖。

3

🐍 A snake wants to eat the eggs! Mother bird stops it with her beak.

蛇想吃蛋！鳥媽媽用嘴喙阻止牠。

4

🐛 Baby birds come out. Parents give them worms.

小鳥孵出，爸媽餵蟲子。

5

🪶 Little birds grow feathers. Parents keep them safe.

長出羽毛，爸媽保護牠們。

6

🕊️ Birds are big and strong. Time to fly away!

長大變強壯，飛走了！

💬 重要句子
Mother birds lay eggs.
When baby birds grow feathers, they leave the nest.
I grow and get smarter / taller / bigger / stronger.

📚 Unit 2「Nate Is Late」

🐍 故事大綱
1

⏰ Nate wakes up at 8:00. Meeting friends at 10:30.

八點起床，約好十點半見朋友。

2

🛁 Nate takes a bath with bubbles at 8:15.

八點十五分泡泡浴。

3

🏃 He leaves home early at 8:30.

八點半提早出門。

4

🐸 It's hot. Nate takes a nap at 9:30.

很熱，九點半睡午覺。

5

😱 It's 10:00! Nate hurries. He doesn't want to be late.

十點了！趕快跑！

6

📚 Friends wait at 10:45. They enjoy the story together!

朋友在等，一起聽故事！

⏱️ 時間表
時間做什麼英文
⏰ 8:00起床wakes up
🛁 8:15洗澡takes a bath
🏃 8:30提早出門leaves early
😴 9:30睡午覺takes a nap
🏃💨 10:00趕快跑hurries
📚 10:45聽故事enjoys story

📚 Unit 3「The Family Garden」

🌱 故事大綱
1

🏡 Beth and Mike want to make a garden.

想做花園！

2

⛏️ Mom and Dad dig. Mike and Grandpa put seeds and plants in the garden.

爸媽挖土，Mike和爺爺種植。

3

🚿 Beth uses a watering can to water the seeds.

Beth 用澆水壺澆水。

4

🌿 Plants grow fast. Vines have buds → become vegetables!

植物長快，藤蔓變蔬菜！

5

🧺 They pick vegetables. They taste good!

採摘蔬菜，好好吃！

6

🎁 They give some to Mrs. White. She is very happy!

送給 White 奶奶，她很開心！

📋 First → Next → Then → Last
First：全家一起做花園。
Next：植物長得很快。
Then：全家採蔬菜。
Last：送蔬菜給 Mrs. White。

📚 Unit 4「The Lost Mitten」

🧤 故事大綱
1

🧤 Lance gets new mittens. He goes out but loses one!

Lance 的新手套掉了！

2

🐭 Five mice find it. They want to hide. They go inside.

五隻老鼠找到，躲進去。

3

🐰 Rabbit wants to sleep. He goes in with the mice.

兔子想睡覺，也鑽進去。

4

🦔 Hedgehog is cold. He goes in too.

刺蝟很冷，也鑽進去。

5

🐻 Bear is tired. She goes in. It's crowded!

熊很累，也進去。好擠！

6

🦗 Cricket goes in. Rip! Snap! POP! Mitten is gone!

蟋蟀也進去。啪！手套炸掉了！

🔗 原因→結果（Cause & Effect）
Lance loses mitten → Mice hide inside
Bear is tired → She goes in the mitten
Cricket goes in → Too crowded → mitten POPS!

📚 Unit 5「Life at Home」

🏠 課文大綱（非小說）
1

🏘️ Life is different now. Houses are bigger with more rooms.

現在生活不同了，房子更大更多房間。

2

🔥→🍳 Past: cooked on a fire. Now: cook on a stove.

以前用火煮，現在用爐子。

3

🧵→🛒 Past: made own clothes. Now: buy in a store.

以前自己做衣服，現在去店裡買。

4

🪣→🚰 Past: washed in a tub. Now: use a sink.

以前用水桶洗碗，現在用水槽。

5

😊 Life now is easier than in the past!

現在的生活比以前容易！

🔄 過去 vs 現在
項目🕰️ 過去✨ 現在
🏠 房子one roombigger / more rooms
🍳 煮飯firestove
👗 衣服made ownbuy in store
🍽️ 洗碗tubsink

📚 Unit 6「A Look at Breakfast」

🍞 課文大綱（非小說）
1

🍞 I like bread for breakfast. Bread is made from wheat.

我喜歡吃麵包，麵包是由小麥做的。

2

🚜 Big machines pick the wheat. Then it is crushed to make flour.

大機器收割小麥，再磨成麵粉。

3

👨‍🍳 The baker makes bread with the flour.

麵包師用麵粉做麵包。

4

🍇 I spread grape jam on my bread. Farmers pick grapes → crushed → make jam.

我在麵包上抹葡萄果醬。農夫採葡萄→榨碎→做果醬。

5

🍊 I drink orange juice. Farmers pick oranges → crushed → make juice.

我喝柳橙汁。農夫採橘子→榨碎→做果汁。

6

🚚 Trucks take the food to stores. People buy the food for a delicious breakfast!

卡車把食物送到商店，人們買來享用美味早餐！

🌾 食物的旅程（Farm to Table）
🍽️ 食物🌱 原料來自🏭 如何製作
🍞 Breadwheat 小麥machines pick → crush → flour → baker makes bread
🍇 Grape Jamgrapes 葡萄farmers pick grapes → crush → jam
🍊 Orange Juiceoranges 橘子farmers pick oranges → crush → juice
💬 重要句子
Bread is made from wheat. 麵包由小麥做成。
Big machines pick the wheat. It is crushed to make flour.
I spread grape jam on my bread. 我在麵包上抹果醬。
People buy the food for a delicious breakfast.

📚 Unit 7「Animals on the Go」

🐘 Nonfiction · 動物的移動方式
1

🐍 Snakes slither on the ground — fast!

蛇在地上滑行，移動很快。

2

🐢 Turtles are slow on land, fast in water.

烏龜陸地慢，水裡游得快。

3

🦀 Crabs crawl across the sand, then dig a hole and hide.

螃蟹爬過沙地，挖洞躲起來。

4

🐛 Insects buzz around and can be noisy.

昆蟲嗡嗡飛，很吵！

5

🐘 Elephants stomp and wave their trunks.

大象跺腳，揮舞象鼻。

Main Idea：Animals move in many ways.

📚 Unit 8「A Tale of a Tail」

🦫 Folktale · 河狸的故事
1

Beaver has a beautiful, fluffy tail. "I have the best tail of all!"

河狸有美麗蓬鬆的尾巴。

2

Beaver chops a tree — it falls on his tail. "My tail is flat!"

砍樹壓扁了尾巴！

3

The sun says: "A flat tail helps you swim fast!"

扁尾巴可以游得快！

4

Beaver carries friends across the lake for a ride!

河狸載朋友過湖！

Cause & Effect：chop → flat → swim fast → carry friends

📚 Unit 9「A Team of Fish」

🐟 Nonfiction · 魚的團隊合作
1

Fish swim in lakes, rivers, and the ocean — alone or with a partner.

魚可以單獨或結伴游。

2

Some swim close in a team and search for food together.

有些成群搜尋食物。

3

The sea is dangerous! In a group, small fish look like one big fish.

成群看起來像大魚，嚇走敵人！

Main Idea：Working in a team helps fish!

📚 Unit 10「Hungry Animals」

🐆 Nonfiction · 動物吃什麼
1

🦛 Hippos eat lots of grass. Squirrels eat seeds and nuts.

河馬吃草，松鼠吃堅果。

2

🐆 Wild animals like cheetahs hunt other animals — very fast!

野生獵豹獵食，跑得很快。

3

🐸 Frogs and toads catch bugs with their long tongues.

青蛙用長舌頭捕蟲。

4

🐻 Bears eat fish, plants, and even people food!

熊吃魚、植物，甚至人的食物！

Main Idea：Animals eat different kinds of food.

📚 Unit 11「Bug Pals」

🦋 Fantasy · 蟲蟲朋友
1

Bee & Ladybug can fly. Grasshopper can jump. Ant runs fast.

各蟲有各自的本領。

2

Poor Caterpillar can't fly, jump, or run fast. He creeps away sadly.

毛毛蟲什麼都不會，難過地爬走。

3

One day, a beautiful bug with gold wings appears. "It's me! Caterpillar!"

出現美麗蝴蝶：「是我毛毛蟲！」

4

"I'm different now, but I'm still your friend!" (Not the same — but still a pal!)

「我變了，但還是你的朋友！」

Caterpillar creeps away → becomes a beautiful Butterfly!

📚 Unit 12「Helper Dogs」

🐕 Nonfiction · 導盲犬
1

Some people can't see. They get help from guide dogs.

導盲犬幫助看不見的人。

2

These dogs are calm, smart, and easy to train.

冷靜、聰明、容易訓練。

3

It takes 2 years to train. The dog learns to stop at a red signal and lead a person safely.

訓練兩年，學習遇紅燈停下、引導人走路。

Main Idea：Guide dogs are great helpers!
